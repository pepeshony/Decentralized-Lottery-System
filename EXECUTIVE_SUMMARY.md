## 💡 Key Features

### 1. Automatic Prize Recording
When a winner is drawn, the prize is automatically recorded with:
- Prize amount
- Claim status
- Block height timestamp

### 2. On-Demand Prize Claiming
Winners can claim prizes at any time:
- No time limits
- Prevents prize loss
- Secure validation
- Automatic history update

### 3. Comprehensive History Tracking
Every ticket purchase updates player history:
- Total lotteries played
- Total amount spent
- Total amount won
- Total amount claimed

### 4. Transparent Queries
Players can query:
- Individual prize status
- Complete participation history
- Claim verification
